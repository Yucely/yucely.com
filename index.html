About Me

Hello, I'm Mayra Yucely Beb Caal from Guatemala. I have a Ph.D. in control and automation from Bourgogne Franche-Comté University (BAC+8). With a background in mechatronic engineering and a Baccalaureatus in Scientiis from Universidad del Valle de Guatemala, I hold an International Master's degree in mechatronic and micro-mechatronic systems from esteemed institutions like Nile University (Egypt), University of Oviedo (Spain), and École Nationale Supérieure de Mécanique et des Microtechniques (France).

I have experience in robotics, computer vision, research, mechanical maintenance, and microscopy. I take pride in my active membership and voluntary contributions to professional organizations such as IEEE, OWSD, SPIE, and OSA.

I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology and making a positive impact in the field of robotics, computer vision, control and automation. Feel free to connect with me to discuss opportunities and collaborate on exciting projects!
